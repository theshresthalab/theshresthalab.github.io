Publications

 
I am a member of the ATLAS collaboration, which publishes about 100 papers each year. To many of these, I contribute through data-taking operations, trigger performance studies, detector calibration and simulation, student & postdoc supervision, and executing other responsibilities. However, below is a list of publications where I have made major contributions.
 
  • "Configuration and performance of the ATLAS b-jet triggers in Run 2" European Physical Journal C, 81, 1087 (2021)
  • "Search for a heavy Higgs boson decaying into a Z boson and another heavy Higgs boson in the ℓℓbb and ℓℓWW final states in pp collisions at √s=13 TeV with the ATLAS detector" European Physics Journal C 81 396 (2021)
  • Combination of searches for Higgs boson pairs in pp collisions at √s=13 TeV with the ATLAS detector" Physics Letter B 800 135103 (2020)
  • "Higgs boson potential at colliders: Status and perspectives" Reviews in Physics 5 100045 (2020)
  • Search for Higgs boson pair production in the dileptonic WWbb channel with the ATLAS detector in pp collisions at √s=13" Phys. Lett. B801 135145 (2020).
  • Search for Higgs boson pair production in the bbWW* final state at √s=13 with the ATLAS detector," JHEP 04 092 (2019)
  • Search for Higgs boson pair production in the WW*WW* decay channel using ATLAS data recorded at √s=13," Journal of High Energy Physics 05 124 (2019)
  • Observation of Higgs boson production in association with a top quark pair at the LHC with the ATLAS detector" Phys. Lett. B 784 (2018) 173-191
  • Search for pair production of vector-like top quarks in events with one lepton, jets, and missing transverse momentum in √s=13 TeV pp collisions with the ATLAS detector" Journal of High Energy Physics 08 052 (2017)
  • Study of the material of the ATLAS inner detector for Run 2 of the LHC" JINST 12 (2017) 12 P12009
  • Charged-particle distributions in √s=13 TeV pp interactions measured with the ATLAS detector at the LHC" Phys. Lett. B758 67 (2016)
  • Performance of b-jet identification in the ATLAS experiment" JINST 11 P04008 (2016)
  • Luminosity determination in pp collisions at √s=8 TeV using the ATLAS detector at the LHC" EPJ C 76 653 (2016)
  • Search for pair production of new heavy quarks that decay into a W boson and a light quark in pp collisions at √s=8 TeV with the ATLAS detector," Phys. Rev. D92 112007 (2015)
  • Observation of a new particle in the search for the Standard Model Higgs boson with the ATLAS detector at the LHC," Phys. Lett. B 716 1-29 (2012)
  • Search for 4th Generation Quarks with the ATLAS Detector at the LHC," S. Shrestha, AIP Conference Proceedings 1560, 86-89 (2013)
